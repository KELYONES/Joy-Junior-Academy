About Our School

Our Story

Joy Miracle School was founded by the local church after observing a deep need for nurturing children in both academics and character. The initiative was not only to provide education but also to support the government’s efforts in reducing unemployment. With several professional teachers within the church, the school was born—an environment where children could grow intellectually and morally. Through dedicated staff, we aim to raise a generation that excels in knowledge and upholds strong values.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures intellect, character, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

To become a leading institution known for academic excellence and moral integrity.
