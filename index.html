Who We Are

Joy Junior Academy is a leading primary school located in Bisunu Sirisia, dedicated to shaping young minds through holistic education. We believe in combining strong academic foundations with moral and social development.

With modern facilities, qualified teachers, and a nurturing environment, we guide learners to achieve excellence in all areas of life.

Learn More About Us

Our Academic Excellence

At Joy Junior Academy, our academic program is aligned with Kenya’s Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). We emphasize practical learning, creativity, and critical thinking from Pre-Primary to Grade 6.

Learners are guided by trained teachers who focus on both academic success and character development. Our goal is to nurture confident, responsible, and future-ready students.

Admissions Open

Enroll your child at Joy Junior Academy and give them a strong foundation for a brighter future. We are currently admitting students in Playgroup, PP1, PP2, and Grades 1–7 under the CBC system.

Our admissions process is simple and parent-friendly. Reach out to us or visit the school to speak with our friendly team and take a tour of the facilities.
